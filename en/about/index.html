Home sourdough baker keeping a scientific baking journal

Dmitry Platonov. I bake sourdough bread at home — rye, wheat, scalded — and keep this blog as an open recipe journal: precise gram weights, temperatures, and iterative recipe versioning.

A strict, reproducible approach: numbers you can double-check and failures you can roll back to a previous version.
